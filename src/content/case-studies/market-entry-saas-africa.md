## The Challenge

DataFlow Solutions, a European SaaS company specialising in data analytics, wanted to expand into Sub-Saharan Africa. They needed market intelligence, regulatory guidance, and local partnerships to enter four target countries simultaneously.

## Our Approach

We delivered a comprehensive market entry programme:

1. **Market Research**: Analysed market size, competitor landscape, and customer readiness in Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, and South Africa.
2. **Regulatory Analysis**: Mapped data protection regulations, licensing requirements, and tax implications for each country.
3. **Partner Identification**: Identified and vetted potential channel partners, resellers, and implementation consultants.
4. **Localisation Strategy**: Advised on pricing, payment methods, and customer support requirements for each market.
5. **Entry Roadmap**: Delivered a phased entry plan with timelines, milestones, and budget estimates.

## The Result

DataFlow Solutions launched in four countries within six months of engagement. They established partnerships with seven local firms and achieved first-year revenue targets. Our ongoing support continues with quarterly market intelligence updates and partner relationship management.
