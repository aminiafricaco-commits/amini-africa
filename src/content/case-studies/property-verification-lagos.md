# Property Verification Saved Our Client $50,000

## Background

Michael, a diaspora African living in the UK, was interested in purchasing a property in Lagos, Nigeria. The seller presented what appeared to be legitimate documents and was asking for a $50,000 deposit.

## The Challenge

Michael could not travel to Lagos to verify the property himself. He needed someone trustworthy to investigate the property and confirm the seller's claims.

## Our Approach

Our team conducted a comprehensive verification including:

- Title deed verification at the land registry
- Physical site inspection of the property
- Neighbor interviews
- Document authenticity check
- Seller background verification

## What We Discovered

Our investigation revealed:

- The title deed was a forgery
- The seller was not the registered owner
- The property was already involved in a legal dispute
- The seller had attempted similar scams before

## The Outcome

We provided Michael with a detailed report including photographs and registry findings. He decided not to proceed with the purchase, potentially saving $50,000.
