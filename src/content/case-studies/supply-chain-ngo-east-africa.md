## The Challenge

HealthBridge International needed to source medical supplies across Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania. They required reliable vendors who could meet quality standards, deliver on time, and provide transparent pricing. Previous attempts at remote vendor management had resulted in delivery delays and quality issues.

## Our Approach

We implemented a comprehensive vendor management programme:

1. **Vendor Identification**: Researched and shortlisted 24 potential suppliers across three countries.
2. **Verification**: Conducted physical inspections of all shortlisted vendors, verifying licences, facilities, quality control processes, and financial stability.
3. **Negotiation**: Facilitated price negotiations, achieving average 18% cost reduction.
4. **Monitoring**: Implemented monthly quality assurance inspections and delivery tracking.
5. **Reporting**: Provided weekly status reports with photographs, delivery confirmations, and quality assessments.

## The Result

HealthBridge achieved 100% delivery compliance across all three countries, reduced procurement costs by 30%, and established long-term relationships with vetted suppliers. The programme has since expanded to include five additional African countries.
