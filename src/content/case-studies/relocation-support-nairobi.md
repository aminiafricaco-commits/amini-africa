# Seamless Relocation for a Family of Four to Nairobi

## Background

Sarah and David decided to move back to Africa after 15 years in Canada. They had two school-age children and needed comprehensive relocation support.

## The Challenge

The family had not lived in Africa for over a decade and needed help with every aspect of their relocation including housing, schools, and settling in.

## Our Approach

We provided end-to-end relocation support:

- Pre-arrival consultation and planning
- Airport pickup and welcome
- Temporary accommodation arrangement
- Property viewings for long-term rental
- School research and tour arrangements
- Utility setup coordination
- Vehicle sourcing assistance
- Area orientation and local guide

## The Outcome

Within two weeks of arrival, the family was settled into their new home, children were enrolled in school, and utilities were fully operational.

Client testimonial: "Amini Africa made our transition seamless. From the moment we landed, everything was taken care of. We could not have done this without them."
