## The Challenge

Michael, a Kenyan diaspora professional based in London, identified a commercial property in Nairobi's Upper Hill area through an online listing. The price was competitive, but Michael had been burned before — a family friend had lost significant money on a property with title disputes.

## Our Approach

Our property verification team conducted a comprehensive due diligence process:

1. **Title Search**: Full search at the Ministry of Lands confirmed clean title with no encumbrances, caveats, or disputes.
2. **Physical Inspection**: Our team visited the site, photographed the property, assessed its condition, and verified boundaries matched the title deed.
3. **Valuation**: Independent valuation confirmed the asking price was fair.
4. **Neighbourhood Assessment**: Evaluation of infrastructure, security, access roads, and future development plans.
5. **Legal Review**: Our partner lawyer reviewed the sale agreement and identified two clauses that needed amendment.

## The Result

Michael proceeded with the purchase with full confidence. The legal amendments saved him from potential liability issues. He saved approximately $120,000 compared to similar properties, thanks to our early identification of a minor structural issue that allowed him to negotiate a price reduction.
