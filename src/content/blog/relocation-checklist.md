# Ultimate Relocation Checklist: Moving to Africa

Relocating to a new country is exciting but can be overwhelming. Use this checklist to ensure nothing is missed during your move to Africa.

## 3 Months Before Moving

- Research your destination country
- Apply for visas and work permits
- Arrange international health insurance
- Start sorting and downsizing belongings
- Research international moving companies

## 2 Months Before Moving

- Notify banks and credit card companies
- Arrange school enrollment for children
- Research housing options
- Set up international bank accounts
- Begin vaccination schedules if needed

## 1 Month Before Moving

- Book flights and accommodation
- Arrange airport pickup
- Cancel or forward utilities
- Notify government agencies of address change
- Prepare essential documents

## 2 Weeks Before Moving

- Confirm moving company arrangements
- Pack essential items for first week
- Notify friends and family of new address
- Confirm accommodation arrangements
- Prepare local currency

## Upon Arrival

- Complete customs and immigration
- Check into accommodation
- Register with local authorities
- Set up local bank account
- Get local SIM card
- Register with embassy

## First Month

- Complete utility setup
- Explore the local area
- Register with local healthcare
- Obtain local driving license
- Join expat and community groups
